Preschool Program (Age 3 to 5)

Our Preschool Program is designed to ignite a love for learning in your child. Through a blend of play-based and structured activities, children explore literacy, numeracy, science, music, and creative arts.

Children in this program benefit from:

  • Daily circle time and themed activities
  • Hands-on exploration and sensory play
  • Outdoor play and physical development sessions
  • Social and emotional skill building
  • Individualized attention with low teacher-to-child ratios

Our qualified educators create a nurturing environment that prepares children for a successful transition to kindergarten while fostering independence, confidence, and curiosity.
