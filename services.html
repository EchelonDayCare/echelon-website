Our Services

At Echelon Day Care, we provide a nurturing and stimulating environment where children grow through play, learning, and social interaction.

Our services include:

  • Infant & Toddler Care: Safe and responsive care for ages 6 months to 2 years.
  • Preschool Program: Engaging curriculum for ages 2 to 5 years to foster early learning and development.
  • After-School Program: Homework help, activities, and fun for children up to 10 years old.
