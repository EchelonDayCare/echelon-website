Echelon Parent Portal

Stay connected with your child's day from anywhere. Our secure online portal gives families real-time updates, photos, attendance check-ins, two-way messaging with educators, and easy online billing — all in one place.

Sign In to the Portal Request Access

Educators and administrators sign in here too. New parents will receive an invitation email after enrollment.

What you can do

📋

Daily Activity Feed

See meals, naps, learning moments, and milestones logged by your child's educators throughout the day.

✅

Attendance & Check-in

Track sign-in and pickup times, and view your child's attendance history at a glance.

📸

Photos & Updates

Receive moments captured during the day, shared securely with your family.

💬

Two-Way Messaging

Message your child's classroom directly — share notes, ask questions, get quick replies.

💳

Online Billing

View invoices, pay tuition online, and download statements anytime.

🔒

Private & Secure

Role-based access keeps each family's information visible only to authorized guardians and staff.

Built for everyone at Echelon

For Parents & Guardians

Daily updates on your child, secure messaging, photo feed, and tuition payments.

For Educators

Quick check-ins, activity logging, photo sharing, and direct family communication.

For Administrators

Manage staff, classrooms, enrollments, invoices, and program-wide reporting.
