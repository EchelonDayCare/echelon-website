About Echelon Day Care

Welcome to Echelon Day Care — where nurturing care meets joyful learning! We are a dedicated team of early childhood educators passionate about creating a safe, stimulating, and inclusive environment for children to grow and thrive.

Our Vision

To build a foundation of lifelong learning by fostering creativity, confidence, and kindness in every child we care for.

Our Mission

  • Provide a secure and loving environment for all children.
  • Encourage curiosity through hands-on learning and play.
  • Support each child’s social, emotional, physical, and cognitive development.

Our Team

Led by experienced educators and caregivers, our staff is trained in child safety, CPR, early childhood development, and emotional intelligence. We take pride in forming strong partnerships with parents and guardians.

Why Choose Us?

  • Bright, clean, and engaging facilities.
  • Daily learning through stories, music, art, and play.
  • Balanced nutrition and outdoor time.
  • Regular updates and photo sharing with parents.
