Hiring process & FAQ

How do I apply?

Click the Apply button on any role to open the application form. We accept resumes (PDF/DOCX) and a short cover note. You will see an on-screen confirmation after submission.

What happens after I apply?

Our HR team reviews applications weekly. If your skills match, we’ll invite you to a short interview. We aim to respond within two weeks.
